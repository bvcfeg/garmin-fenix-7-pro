How to Install the Garmin Fenix 7 Pro Update

Updating your Garmin Fenix 7 Pro is simple. Just follow these steps:

  1. Ensure your Fenix 7 Pro is fully charged.
  2. Connect to a Wi-Fi network or pair it with your smartphone running the Garmin Connect app.
  3. Open the Garmin Connect app or Garmin Express on your PC.
  4. Check for the update and follow the on-screen instructions.
  5. Once completed, restart your Fenix 7 Pro to finalize the update.
