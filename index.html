What's New in the Garmin Fenix 7 Pro Update?

The new update for the Garmin Fenix 7 Pro brings several exciting enhancements:

How to Install the Garmin Fenix 7 Pro Update

Updating your Garmin Fenix 7 Pro is a simple process. Follow these steps:

  1. Ensure your Fenix 7 Pro is fully charged before starting the update.
  2. Connect the device to Wi-Fi or a paired mobile device with the Garmin Connect app installed.
  3. Open the Garmin Connect app on your phone or go to the Garmin Express software on your computer.
  4. Check for the available update and follow the on-screen instructions to install it.
  5. Once installed, restart your device to complete the process.
